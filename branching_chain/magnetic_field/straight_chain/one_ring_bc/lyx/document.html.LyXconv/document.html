Let now consider the straight semi-infinite chain of rings with delta-coupling imposed at each vertex. Wave function components on j-th ring are as follows:
ψj(x) = (Cj + eikx + Cj − e − ikx)e − iΦx
φj(x) = (Dj + eikx + Dj − e − ikx)eiΦx
At the boundary points those components have following values
for ψj
ψj(0) = Cj +  + Cj − , 
ψj(π) = (Cj + eikπ + Cj − e − ikπ)e − iΦπ, 
and for φj
φj(0) = Cj +  + Cj − , 
φj(π) = (Cj + eikπ + Cj − e − ikπ)e − iΦπ.
As delta-copling demands the components values at the contact poins of the ring must be equal to each other:
ψj(0) = φj(0)
and
ψj(π) = φj(π).
Solving this pair of equations we arrive at the relation between coeffitients:
Dj +  = (eikπ − e − ikπ) − 1e − iΦπ(Cj + (ei(k − Φ)π − e − i(k − Φ)π) + Cj − e − ikπ(e − iΦπ − eiΦπ))), 
Dj −  = (eikπ − e − ikπ) − 1e − iΦπ(Cj + eikπ(e − iΦπ − eiΦπ) + Cj − (e − i(k + Φ)π − ei(k + Φ)π)).
